Company Introduction

ArkanFelez Manufacturing and Industrial Company (Limited Liability) ArkanFelez Co., a producer of all kinds of industrial machinery, including the production line of food, pharmaceutical, cosmetic, chemical and...It was established in the year 1363 under number 1170 in the Register of Documents of Qazvin Province under the management of Mr. Mohammad Ali Rahmani, with activities in the field of designing and producing machinery for the food and chemical industries. During the following years, this company has expanded its activities as a production and industrial group of metal elements by purchasing Foladan production and industrial companies (with registration number 1150) and Arkan Alborz plan (with registration number 4232) and has so far succeeded in manufacturing machines.There have been many. ArkanFelez production and industrial group, as the first manufacturer of fully automatic hinged doors for metal doors, the first manufacturer of industrial vinegar production machines, the first manufacturer of vacuum homogenizer mixers under Chroma design, Germany, the oldest and largest manufacturer of machines for the production of sauces (mayonnaise, ketchup, etc.) with the largest number and variety of production has contributed greatly to the equipping and modernization of the country's factories and has made them largely redundant from foreign counterparts, and by exporting to neighboring countries, it has contributed to the waving of the flag of our country's industry beyond the borders of Iran.The continuous improvement of product quality, matching products with the needs of buyers and agility of the collection in after-sales services are among the most important goals of this group.
